AMS (Pre-Shift Examination System)

AMS is my way of rewriting a story I never want anyone else to live. Seeing my dad come home every day, exhausted, his health breaking down was a nightmare. AMS is my promise that tomorrow will be safer, and tragedy prevented. It’s more than a project; it’s my way of saying, "You’ll make it home, Dad. Always."

  • Key Features: Hardware device with sensors measuring metrics like alcohol levels, pulse, and temperature; Mobile app for safety engineers with real-time data and notifications.
  • Traction: Secured $5,000 funding for implementation in government offices and clinics; ongoing international negotiations for expansion.

QarapaiymEmdeu (Robot-Therapist)

QarapaiymEmdeu is an AI-powered robot therapist that bridges the gap in healthcare access. It provides personalized health recommendations and online medical services, ensuring timely care for all.

  • Key Features: AI doctor chatbot for tailored advice; mobile app offering medical certificates, sick leave notes, and integrated medication orders.
  • Traction: Installed in 18 clinics, screening over 2,000 patients; showcased to EAEU ministers at Digital Almaty.

RESQ (Gas Analyzer)

RESQ is a comprehensive gas analyzer designed to enhance workplace safety by detecting harmful gases and identifying fire and oil leak sources using AI-driven technology.

  • Key Features: Hardware for gas detection; computer vision for monitoring protective equipment and identifying fire/oil leaks.
  • Traction: Finalist in industry competitions; developed with support from top institutions.

3GIS (Fleet Management Platform)

3GIS is a platform designed to optimize fleet management for safety, efficiency, and cost-effectiveness. From detecting driver drowsiness to preventing accidents, it’s built to make roads safer.

  • Key Features: Driver behavior monitoring; public transit integration for safety and efficiency.
  • Traction: Winner of multiple startup competitions; implemented in oil and gas fleets and public transit systems.
